So, what are our sonnets about and what do we hope to achieve?

The poems is about showing the side of love no one likes to see. It could be a breakup, a one-sided love, and a love that was never meant to be. With this, we hope that people understand that based on these poems that love is not always the cliche fantasy like in movies or storybook and could actually be cruel and heartless despite the name. It is also a hope that we show that despite all that had happened, the poems show the person's perspective in the relationship. It shows how they can see love as a brittle piece of glass that can shatter one way or another or how it can be a gamble you can lose to someone else. These sonnet collection hopes to show the sad realities of love and the unspoken imperfections of it.
