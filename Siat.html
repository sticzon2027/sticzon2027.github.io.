A Petrarchan Sonnet

A Sacrifice For Joy

Happier by Marshmello

Every morning, my love shines bright like a broken flame

But we always had our arguments and setbacks

And fought from dogs to simple laughs

Oh how I wish we never knew the way our love would be untamed

Every evening, I’m filled with shame

I thought of you during my hot baths

We always pretended there were no traps

And now I wish we could swim far away from the pain

A minute has passed and my mind wants to change

Cause this just doesn't feel right to me

But I feel you must know

Even though it's strange

I want to see you feel glee

But know I'll have to go

A Shakesperean Sonnet

Reminiscing My Ex-Lover

The One That Got Away by Katy Perry

I met you in summer

Driving in your mustang to Radiohead

Never one without the other

“Like June and Johnny Cash” you said

10 years later, I’m listening to our song

And reminiscing our matching tattoo

I always thought our life was lifelong

Who knew I’d be losing you

Though with a million rings I can’t lift my curse

And buy a time machine with all my money

I hope in another universe

You were here for me

So I don’t have to say

You’re the one that went faraway
