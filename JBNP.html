Love: The play

It's Over Isn't It by Steven Universe

Their meaningless love was a play, until you

Love was a childish competition, who would she choose

After all those years, I fell to the unforeseen, I fell to you, I fell behind

You won our foolish game of love, yet i can’t get it out of my mind

She loved thee with a red heart, though the life of heart out, it got blue

It's over, isn't it, it's all over due

Yet why am I still attached, why is my heart in a bind

I, placed upon a spotlight, bright as the morning sun

Fought in wars, for freedom, but all i wanted was to be heard

But who am I now, a meaningless existence with a world without her?

I was stupid and trivial, and questioned her words

No point in a meaningless rant when everything’s done

A Shakesperean Sonnet

Paper Castles

Burn by Phillipa Soo

I love you, as never ending as a phrase

Your letters, a fortress of love, paper castles

I fell helpless to your sweet gaze, your loving praise

bound to you by shackles

I love you, a phrase as past as “been”

A devastating quake, as our love quivers

Our chapels crumble to your sin

The great divine, could not even be your line of silver

Our love, tested, displayed to the world in a booth

“I shall create my own legacy” Actions influenced by some foolish desire

Your words hold power, but do they hold the truth?

Your paragraphs hold structure, but can they withstand fire?

You come from a land of no return

Now I watch as our castles Burn
